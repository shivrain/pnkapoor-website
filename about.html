— Our story

A standard set in 1996.
A company built for now.

1996
The standard is set
PN Kapoor (the elder) set the family rule we still run on: quality is a non-negotiable on the factory floor, not a marketing line — and a small brand gets the same seriousness as a big one. A standard the family kept and proved, generation after generation.
2018
The decision to build
The next generation saw what the legacy industry kept missing: India's cosmetic landscape was being rewired by D2C founders that traditional MOQs locked out entirely. That was the moment to build — a modern company designed around the standard, and around them.
2022
Built, on purpose
Chayan Kapoor, 3rd generation, builds PN Kapoor Healthcare — a modern, GMP-certified factory in Mayapuri, stood up deliberately for today's founders. Engineered differently from day one: 500-unit MOQs, in-house R&D, 12-week timelines. The first D2C orders land within months.
Year 1 · ₹1Cr+
The standard pays off
Year-one revenue crosses ₹1 Cr — and every brand we onboard reorders. The standard earns its keep: founders don't just need product, they need a partner that takes their earliest stage as seriously as their biggest.
2026 · Today
40+ brands and counting

— What we believe

Three principles. Non-negotiable.

01

Founders aren't small clients.

They're future big clients we get to grow with. We don't hide our best chemists behind 5,000-unit minimums. The same R&D team that develops formulas for our largest accounts works on first-batch founder orders.

02

Quality is a floor, not a ceiling.

FDA, ISO 22716, GMP, HALAL — these are starting points. Every batch gets a COA. Every formula is stability-tested. Every shipment is documented to the standard that holds up under audit, not just under marketing.

03

Your IP is your IP.

NDAs signed before the first technical call. Bespoke formulations belong to your brand, not our library. If you ever choose to leave, formula docs transfer to your next manufacturer without friction.
