01.
Weeks 1 — 2
— Brief & Specification

It starts with a conversation.

A 30-minute discovery call. A WhatsApp note. A photo of a competitor product you want to beat. There's no "correct format" for a brief — we'll extract what we need by asking the right questions.

What we do
  • Run a 30–60 min discovery call covering category, positioning, target customer, and budget
  • Review any reference products, competitor formulas, or constraints you bring
  • Convert the conversation into a written brief + feasibility report within 5 business days
  • Identify any regulatory red flags before you commit to anything
What you do
  • Sign an NDA — we send it before the first call
  • Share your rough brief, even if it's "I want a niacinamide serum that's better than X"
  • Decide which categories and how many SKUs you want at launch
  • Confirm directional retail price and target margin

02.
Weeks 3 — 7
— R&D Formulation

Two to three variants. You pick.

Our chemists develop multiple variants of your formula. We don't pull a base off the shelf and slap your label on it. Every product starts from scratch using your brief, your budget, and your performance targets.

What we do
  • Develop 2–3 formula variants exploring different actives, textures, or scent profiles
  • Conduct preliminary stability and pH testing on each variant
  • Send physical samples (15ML / 30ML) to your address — typically 50g across variants
  • Walk you through the trade-offs of each variant on a video call
  • Iterate based on your feedback — usually 1–2 rounds of refinement
What you do
  • Test the samples for at least 7 days — both yourself and a small group
  • Document feedback on texture, scent, absorption, skin feel
  • Pick a direction (or ask us to merge the best of two variants)
  • Approve the final formula in writing before pilot batch begins

03.
Weeks 8 — 11
— Pilot Batch

500 real units. Real packaging.

This is the moment your brand becomes physical. We manufacture 500 units in the approved formula, in your final packaging, with your label artwork. Real product. Shelf-ready. Ready to ship.

What we do
  • Procure raw materials and packaging components from approved vendors
  • Manufacture 500 units in our GMP-certified facility
  • Run accelerated stability test (mimics 12 months in 4 weeks)
  • Apply your label artwork to bottles (or full secondary packaging)
  • Quality-control each batch with checks documented in COA
What you do
  • Finalize label artwork and submit print-ready files by Week 8
  • Approve final packaging mockups (we'll send 2-3 pre-production samples)
  • Make the production deposit (typically 50% of total batch cost)
  • Plan your launch dates around the Week 12 shipping window

04.
Week 12 +
— Shipped & Scale

Out the door. Onto your shelf.

Your batch clears QC, dispatches from Mayapuri, and lands at your warehouse. From here, every re-order is faster — formulation locked, packaging vendors aligned, raw material pipeline established.

What we do
  • Final QC pass — every batch gets visual, physical, and microbial checks
  • Pack and dispatch within 5 business days of QC clearance
  • Send shipping confirmation, COA, and complete documentation packet
  • Hold safety stock of your key raw materials for fast re-orders
  • Schedule a Month 1 review call to plan re-orders and scale
What you do
  • Final payment cleared upon QC sign-off (50% remaining)
  • Confirm shipping address and any specific logistics requirements
  • Launch your product — we cheer you on from the sidelines
  • Send re-order forecast when you have one — even directional helps us

— What you actually need to start

Three things to bring. That's it.

Founders often delay reaching out because they think they need a complete brand brief, a designed package, and a market study. You don't. Bring these, and we'll figure out the rest with you.

①

A rough idea

"Niacinamide serum, oily skin, under ₹399 retail" is already enough for our first call. We'll structure it into a brief together.

②

Your budget range

Even a directional answer — ₹2L? ₹5L? ₹15L? — helps us scope the right number of SKUs and packaging tier from day one.

③

A launch goal

"Launching at a beauty event in March" or "selling on Nykaa from June" — we backtrack the 12-week timeline from your goal.
