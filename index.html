— How it works

Five steps. ~Ten weeks.

From the moment we finalise your products to the moment your finished goods ship from our facility. Documented, predictable, quick, and built to fit founder timelines.

①
Week 1

Share your brief

Discovery call, requirement gathering. We translate your product vision into a spec document for our R&D team.

②
Week 2 – 3

R&D formulates

Bespoke formulations developed & sent to you for approval. You test and provide your feedback.

③
Week 4 – 5

Re-iterate & approve

R&D re-iterates as per your feedback. Updated samples sent to you — you test, choose and approve.

④
Week 6 – 8

Documentation & sourcing

Final documentation and sourcing of raw material and packaging for production.

⑤
Week 9 – 12

Production & dispatch

Production, packaging, QC clearance, and final dispatch.
